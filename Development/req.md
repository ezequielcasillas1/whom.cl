SUMMARY (Plain and Honest)

In the New Testament (NKJV), “whom” most often appears when Scripture is emphasizing:

Jesus as the sent One

Jesus as the object of faith

God’s deliberate choice of persons

Identity and allegiance

The most theologically weighty “whom” verses are:

Matthew 16:15

John 6:29

John 1:33

John 15:26

Romans 8:29

Colossians 1:14
